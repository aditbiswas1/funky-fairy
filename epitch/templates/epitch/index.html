ABOUT E-PITCH

E-pitch is the annual flagship B-Plan contest of NITK, Surathkal organised by EFOREA. E-Pitch provides the perfect platform for budding student entrepreneurs to launch their innovative and creative business ideas. Every year E-Pitch receives over 150 entries with the winners of E-Pitch being offered incubation support at NITK-STEP. The winners also bag a direct entry to Next Big Idea, one of the country’s largest B-Plan contests organized by NSRCEL, IIM B. Open to all college students, E-Pitch provides a perfect head-start for student entrepreneurs.

Prizes

1st prize: Rs. 50,000
2nd prize: Rs. 35,000
3rd prize: Rs. 20,000

Participation prizes of Rs. 5,000 to the next 5 teams.
Incubation at NITK STEP with seed funding of Rs. 10,00,000 to the top 3 teams.





Rules and Regulations

  • Only students currently pursuing undergraduate or post-graduate courses in colleges with a valid College ID card can participate.
  • A team can have a maximum of 4 members (Interdisciplinary teams are encouraged).
  • The proposed business idea must be the original work of the team making the submission.
  • An individual can be part of only one team.
  • The business plan must not exceed 2000 words and should be sent to epitch.eforea@gmail.com in PDF Format by 11:59 PM, January 22nd 2014.
  • This cover page should include names and contact details of the team members and name of the idea.
  • Shortlisted teams will be notified well before the Final Round – February 5th 2014.
  • Only one submission per participant is allowed.
  • All decisions of the Organizing Committee of E-Pitch 2013 are final and binding.
